WITCH-HUNT

APRILLIA

APRILLIA (Stephanie Aprillia Alist) is a Singaporean singer-rapper-songwriter who first gained attention for her bold vocals and playful rap through K-pop and hip-hop covers on YouTube and TikTok. She has since contributed her vocals and songwriting to pop-rap collaborations and K-pop demo pitches, working with international producers virtually. In 2025, she was selected as one of eight mentees for the *SCAPE Aspiring Musician Residency Programme to receive mentorship from music industry professionals and showcase her original demos to the Singapore music scene. Influenced by next-gen pop-rap icons like JENNIE and Doja Cat, APRILLIA fuses hip-hop, K-pop, and dark, danceable beats into infectious, high-energy, story-driven bangers. She now makes her bold debut with “WITCH-HUNT.”

THE PURGE

"WITCH-HUNT" is a visceral cleansing of the social rot. The "witches" are not supernatural; they are the fraudulent souls and fake acquaintances masquerading as friends. APRILLIA assumes the role of the inquisitor—tracking the wolves in sheep’s clothing until they burn in the webs of lies they wove.
